minis

What is ASU Minis?

This is an upper and lower classmen program established in 2013, in hopes to bring a sense of bonding and friendship between all members in ASU outside of ASU general meetings and events. It is a great way to branch out and meet more students and members in ASU – we hope you’ll be able to meet people who you not normally see in your classes.

Who is it for?

Everyone! Not just freshmen or new members, but old members are encouraged to join as well! It is a great way to meet new people, broaden your horizons, and come out of your comfort zone as you come across new ideas and people that you normally wouldn’t have.

How do I Join?

It’s easy! You just need to sign up through our website. We’ll need you to fill out a quick form and once you click “submit”, that’s all there is to it. From there, we’ll randomly split you into groups and within each group, there were be a spectacular Mini Head that will guide your adventures!

Do I have to join ASU Minis to be a part of ASU?

Nope. ASU Minis is not a requirement to be a part of our ASU family! If you aren’t interested in joining the program, that’s fine. You are still a member of ASU and we welcome you to come to our GMs, outings, events, etc with open arms! If you have a friend who is a part of a mini’s group and they are doing something super cool during the weekend, you are also more than welcomed to tag along!

about asu

Asian Student Union (ASU) is a cultural group on campus at Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts.

Through general meetings and events, we seek to promote awareness of Asian American culture while making friends and have fun along the way!
